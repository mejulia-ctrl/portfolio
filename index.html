About Me

Art through a
digital lens

Hi! I'm Ella, a second-year Computer Studies student at Naga College Foundation. I explore art through digital tools — from generative visuals to thoughtful UI design.

I believe technology and art aren't opposites. Every line of code can be a brushstroke, and every interface is a canvas waiting to be shaped with intention and care.

Outside of screens, I play ukulele and dream of building something of my own someday. 🎵
