Baking 101

Tips for Beginners

🌡️

Room Temp Ingredients

Always use room temperature butter and eggs. Cold ingredients don't mix well and can affect your cake texture.

⚖️

Measure Accurately

Baking is science! Use a kitchen scale for best results. Too much flour = dry cake. Too little = flat cake.

🚫

Don't Overmix

Once you add flour, mix just until combined. Overmixing develops gluten and makes your cake tough and dense.

🔥

Preheat Your Oven

Always preheat! Putting cake batter into a cold oven will mess up the rise and baking time.

🧁

Cool Before Frosting

Let your cake cool completely before adding frosting, or it will melt right off!

📝

Read the Full Recipe First

Before you start, read the whole recipe so you know what's coming and have everything ready.
